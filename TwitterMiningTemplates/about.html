What Is This System?


Our system revolves around handling data on the social media website Twitter. Our goal is to make Twitter a safer environment for not just youngsters but anyone who wishes to use it. Terrorism, Hate and Offensive Speech and Cyber Trolling are a major issue regarding social media websites. These issues can be brought to light and reported through our system systematically. We have used the Twitter API Tweepy for getting the tweets and retweets in different ways as shown in the working below.



How To Use This System?


Steps: 1. Sign up as a new user.

                 2. Login using your creditials.

                                                   3. Choose one of the three radio buttons available.

                                                                                      4. Results will be shown as offensive or non offensive in dataframe table.

                             5. Tweets can be cleared or reported.
